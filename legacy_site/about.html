About us

Astemes provides expertise in design and development of automated test and measurement systems. Our mission is to enable innovation and excellence by providing world class technical expertise. We value software craftmanship and continuously work to improve and widen our offerings by actively participating in the international community.

We are an official NI (National Instruments) Partner and have high certified competency within the NI toolchain, including the Certified LabVIEW Architect (CLA) and Certified TestStand Developer (CTD) certifications. The CLA is the highest level of certification offered for NI LabVIEW. We are also highly effective working in Python and comfortable with Java and C++.

We value transparency and always deliver to the best of our ability. While we do want our customers to come back, we have no intention to lock anyone in. Source code and documentation is, for this reason, always provided as part of our deliveries.

Astemes is based in Pietarsaari, Finland.

About NI Partner Network 

The NI Partner Network is a global community of domain, application, and overall test development experts working closely with NI to meet the needs of the engineering community. NI Partners are trusted solution providers, systems integrators, consultants, product developers, and services and sales channel partners skilled across a wide range of industries and application areas. Learn more and connect with the NI Partner Network at ni.com/partners.
