Solutions

Systems we build

An automated test system is software and hardware tightly integrated to make a set of measurements which are evaluated to provide a test result.

Development Test

Flexible, user-configurable systems for product development. By automating repetitive tests, more time can be spent innovating.

Production Test

Reliable and simple-to-operate systems for manufacturing quality assurance. Reduce time spent on testing while proving product quality through measurements.

Embedded Software Test

Hardware-in-the-loop testing with real hardware and physical signals. Integrates with CI systems for automated regression testing.

Monitoring & Life-Time Test

Systems that gather operational data for predictive maintenance. Built to be reliable even when deployed in uncertain environments.

How we work

A clear process,
transparent delivery

We use industry-standard technologies and proven frameworks to produce high-quality software efficiently. By leveraging existing open-source and proprietary tools for generic tasks, we can focus on your specific domain problems, reducing maintenance cost and total cost of ownership over the system lifecycle.

We believe in being transparent with progress and raising potential issues as early as possible. After commissioning, a support plan can be defined to ensure the system continues to deliver value over time.

01

Define & Assess

Needs and requirements are gathered. A pre-study may clarify scope. The outcome is a verified understanding and a solution proposal.

02

Plan & Align

Milestones and deliverables are defined. A feedback cadence is agreed upon before any development begins.

03

Develop & Deliver

Incremental development with transparent progress reporting. Potential issues raised immediately, not at the end.

04

Commission & Support

System goes live. An optional support plan ensures the system is maintained and continues to provide value long-term.

About

Who we are

Astemes provides expertise in the design and development of automated test and measurement systems. Our mission is to enable innovation and excellence by providing world class technical expertise. We value software craftmanship and continuously work to improve and widen our offerings by actively participating in the international community.

We value transparency and always deliver to the best of our ability. While we do want our customers to come back, we have no intention to lock anyone in. Source code and documentation is, for this reason, always provided as part of our deliveries.
